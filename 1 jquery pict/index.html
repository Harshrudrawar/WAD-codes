About PICT

Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT) is a premier institute for computer education. Established in 1983, PICT has been consistently ranked among the top engineering colleges in India.

Our mission is to provide quality education and create a conducive environment for holistic development, nurturing future leaders in the field of technology.
